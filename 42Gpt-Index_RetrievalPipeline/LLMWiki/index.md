## [L_incubateur de 42 Paris/L_incubateur de 42 Paris.md] L’incubateur de 42 Paris
The 42 Paris Incubator, located at STATION F, supports entrepreneurs who are alumni of the 42 network in launching and scaling their projects. The program includes monthly workshops led by experts, group coaching, personalized coaching sessions, access to the STATION F ecosystem, networking opportunities, and a Demo Day at the end of the 9-month program. Eligibility is limited to teams with at least one co-founder who has completed the Common Core at any 42 campus, and applications are open continuously but require validation of the Common Core before entry. Remote participation is not allowed as the incubator is a physical space and community.
